Read Biography

I have been singing and performing my whole life. I used to tell my mom that I would someday be a famous singer, just like Celine Dion. When I'm on the stage, I am home. It's a beautiful feeling. I write songs that I would want to listen to. I love catchy pop songs, banging hip hop beats, alternative synth, smooth r&b vocals. I like to believe that I can infuse everything I love about music together to make something unique and great. My lyrics are my thoughts.. Everything crazy running around in my head, looking to escape through my verses. Being an artist is beautiful because I don't have to be anyone but me! I can let people into my craziness, and it's okay because it's art. If you love to dance, if you love catchy songs, if you love cats, or all of the above; stay tuned. I've got a whole lot more to share with you.
